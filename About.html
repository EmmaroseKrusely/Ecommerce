Company Story

Jane Doe is the CEO of the Only Ferns company, the reason she started the company is so
that people who love plants can get them sent to their door. She works with farms that grow
flowers and plants and through the Only Ferns website you buy the plants and the farms can
get extra profit. Only Ferns has been in business since September, 2020, and their goal
is to help farms acquire extra money. Jane Doe has had experience with working with people
who own farmland and grow plants on that land, and knows that it's hard to find people who
are willing to come out and buy the plants themselves, so she started this business.

USP

There are people out in the world who want to have plants around their house or growing on
their front lawns, but don't want to go out and buy the plants from gardeners who grow
the plants on their farms. That's why through the Only Ferns website, you order the plants
and they get sent to you from the gardeners on the farm without you needing to go
out and get them yourself. You sign up and get the plants sent straight to you.
